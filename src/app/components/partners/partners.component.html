D&B Business Reports

D&B Business Reports are renowned for the up-to-date company and credit information they provide on over 40 million businesses worldwide. Use D&B Reports to find the financial, credit and corporate background information you need to make your credit decisions, sales calls and other business activities more profitable. Over 90 percent of the Business Week Global 1000 rely on D&B as a trusted partner to help them Decide With Confidence.

Available Reports include:

  1. The U.S. D&B Comprehensive Report (CR), featuring D&B's proprietary statistical scoring along with business, payment and financial information all in one report. Use the CR to see how a U.S. company has performed in the past, as well as to predict how it will perform in the future.
  2. The D&B Business Information Report (BIR), a key source for information on a firm's identity, operations, profitability and stability.
  3. The D&B Company Profile, featuring vital business statistics such as address and telephone number, executive names and title, year and location of incorporation, annual sales figures and number of employees. Use a D&B Company Profile to help you qualify leads, locate sources of supply, and identify potential customers.
